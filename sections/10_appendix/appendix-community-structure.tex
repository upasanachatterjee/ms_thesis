Table~\ref{tab:community-structure} presents the full community structure obtained from Louvain community detection on the topic tag co-occurrence graph described in Section~\ref{sec:experimental-design}. Each community groups semantically related topic tags that frequently co-occur within articles. The 15 detected communities range from single-topic clusters (e.g., Community~8: LGBT Rights) to large thematic groupings containing over 80 tags (e.g., Community~9: International Affairs and Climate).
